Accommodations

Hotel — March 6 night

We fly into MCO on Saturday March 6 and check in for one night before boarding the Carnival Venezia at Port Canaveral on Sunday March 7. Port Canaveral is about 1 hour east of MCO, so staying close to the port makes the morning embarkation easy.

Plan is to use Uber / rideshare or a one-way van rental to get from MCO → hotel → port. No need to rent a car for the whole week.

🎯 Port Canaveral / Cocoa Beach
Best option — walk or short ride to the cruise terminal. Easy morning embarkation. Hotels like Residence Inn Cape Canaveral, Hampton Inn Cocoa Beach, or Fairfield by Marriott.
🔶 Cocoa / Viera
Midpoint between MCO and the port. Slightly cheaper, ~30 min Uber to terminal. More hotel variety.
🔷 Lake Nona / East Orlando
Closest to MCO, ~45–55 min to port. Worth it only if arriving late and want minimal ground transfer from airport.

💡 Tip: With 9 people we'll likely need 3–4 hotel rooms. Check for suite hotels or properties with multiple connecting rooms. Two Uber XLs or a van shuttle from MCO to the hotel works well for this group size.
