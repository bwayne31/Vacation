Itinerary

Full trip timeline

Friday, March 5
Fargo crew hits the road
Nolan, Gavin & Mason drive from NDSU toward home (Rochester or MSP). If flying MSP: park at airport and stay nearby overnight.
Saturday, March 6
Fly day — everyone to MCO
Rochester crew carpools to departure airport. All 9 land in Orlando by 5:00 PM. Uber or van to Holiday Inn Express Cape Canaveral. Check in, dinner together, get excited.
Sunday, March 7
Embarkation day 🚢
Check out of hotel, head to Port Canaveral. Board the Carnival Venezia. We're officially on vacation.
March 7–14
7 nights at sea
Caribbean ports, sea days, good food, and family time on the Carnival Venezia.
Saturday, March 14
Disembark & fly home
Off the ship in the morning. Head to MCO. All flights depart no earlier than 1:00–2:00 PM. Everyone back home that evening.
